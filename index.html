ABOUT THE PRACTICE

At CB Bio, we are dedicated to helping individuals improve their movement, strength, and overall quality of life through evidence-based biokinetics. Our practice specializes in injury rehabilitation, chronic condition management, and performance enhancement for all ages and activity levels. We combine clinical expertise with personalized care, ensuring each client receives a tailored program designed to restore function, prevent injury, and support long-term wellness. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing a medical condition, or simply striving to move better, our goal is to guide you every step of the way.

Claudia Badenhorst Biokineticists was founded by Claudia Badenhorst in January 2024, after working as a Biokineticist in the Fourways area since 2019. With a strong passion for helping people move and feel better, Claudia decided it was time to create a dedicated space to call home—thus, CB Bio was born. Located in the heart of Fourways on Kingfisher Drive, just around the corner from Fourways High School, CB Bio offers a welcoming, well-equipped, and spacious environment designed to support patients of all ages and needs. In addition to our main practice, our Biokineticists also consult with pediatric patients at Langhams Lifestyle Experience, where we work as part of a multi-disciplinary team focused on child development.

Among the many areas of interest listed on our WHAT WE DO page, you’ll see we have a special interest in Women’s Health and proudly run weekly ladies’ exercise classes that promote strength, balance, and overall wellness in a supportive group setting, under the supervision of a Biokineticist. Whether you're recovering from an injury, looking to improve performance, or simply want to move better and feel healthier, CB Bio is here for you. From rehab to prehab, kids to seniors, athletes to executives—we can’t wait to welcome you into the CB Bio family.

Hi, I’m Claudia Badenhorst, a passionate Biokineticist based in Fourways. I completed my degree in Human Movement Science at North-West University, followed by my honours in Biokinetics at the University of the Witwatersrand. I've been practicing since 2019 and proudly founded CB Bio in 2024. Sport and movement have always played a central role in my life. Growing up, I earned provincial colours in both hockey and athletics and represented South Africa internationally in athletics.
